Process Systems Engineering Research Group

Advancing intelligent, sustainable, and resilient process systems.

Welcome to PSE@LSU, a research group at Louisiana State University focused on optimization, process control, data-driven modeling, and decision-making for complex chemical, energy, and manufacturing systems.

What We Do

Research at the intersection of engineering, computing, and sustainability

Systems Optimization

Mathematical programming, scheduling, planning, and uncertainty-aware decision support for complex process systems.

Process Control

Model predictive control, monitoring, and automation strategies for safe and reliable operation.

AI for Engineering

Hybrid modeling, machine learning, digital twins, and interpretable analytics for process design and operation.

Our Group

Collaborative, rigorous, and impact-driven.

PSE@LSU brings together faculty, graduate students, undergraduate researchers, and collaborators to solve high-impact problems in chemical engineering and related fields. We welcome motivated students and partners interested in systems research.
